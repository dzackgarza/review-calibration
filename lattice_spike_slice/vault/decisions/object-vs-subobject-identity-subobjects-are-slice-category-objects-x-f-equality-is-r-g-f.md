# Object vs subobject identity

A sublattice is the pair (A, f: A ↪ B) where f.codomain() is the parent.
Equality on subobjects is keyed by (R, G, f), not bare Gram data.

Constructors must not smuggle raw ambient coordinate rows as if they were morphisms.
A lattice is defined by a Gram matrix; a sublattice by a lattice and an embedding into its parent.
